# Plan 04-01

## Objective

Create the first dedicated solutions surface for the site by defining a bilingual solution-content structure and rendering an overview page for the three confirmed directions: industrial automation solutions, software development, and technical integration.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Users can open `/zh/solutions` and `/en/solutions` without falling back to the generic placeholder page
- The page clearly shows the three solution directions
- The page keeps a visible inquiry route and a product-center route
- The content model is structured enough to support deeper CTA and transition work in 04-02
