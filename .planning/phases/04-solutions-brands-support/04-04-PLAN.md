# Plan 04-04

## Objective

Build a dedicated bilingual support page that frames service capability clearly, exposes resource-access paths, and turns support browsing into a clean lead-in to inquiry.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Users can open a dedicated support page instead of a generic placeholder route
- The page explains the company support scope with clear technical-support and guidance modules
- Users can find clear resource-entry paths for catalog request, document/material access, and project-side support intake
- The page keeps inquiry and next-step routes visible so support content does not dead-end
