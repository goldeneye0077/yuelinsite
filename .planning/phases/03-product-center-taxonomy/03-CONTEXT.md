# Phase 3 Context

## Goal

Build the product-center information architecture around five top-level families while keeping the industrial-sensor internals aligned with the reference product taxonomy from the provided source page.

## Scope

- Define a reusable bilingual product taxonomy data model
- Keep the five top-level families aligned with the confirmed business framing
- Sync industrial-sensor subgroup structure from the reference product page
- Separate safety protection sensors and laser ranging sensors into their own primary families
- Give the current product-center route a taxonomy preview without yet building the final landing page or category pages

## Inputs Already Confirmed

- Top-level product families:
  - Industrial sensors
  - Safety protection sensors
  - Laser ranging sensors
  - Linear guides and modules
  - Pneumatic components
- Reference source for sensor taxonomy: `https://www.hyfcn.com/product.html`
- Phase 2 already established bilingual shell, placeholder continuity, and inquiry entry points
- The product center must remain bilingual and fit the existing Precision Industrial language

## Known Constraints

- The reference page is strongly sensor-oriented, so linear-guide and pneumatic structures need a first-pass inferred taxonomy for now
- Product cards and detail-ready UI belong to later Phase 3 plans
- Real product imagery and exact model data are still pending

## Phase Structure

- `03-01`: Structured taxonomy and bilingual product data model
- `03-02`: Product center landing page plus the five top-level category pages
- `03-03`: Industrial sensor subcategory navigation and listing templates
- `03-04`: Representative product card/detail components with inquiry CTA
