# Phase 2 Validation Targets

## Requirements In Scope

- `HOME-01`: First screen explains what the company does
- `HOME-02`: Homepage surfaces strengths, major product directions, and cooperation brands
- `HOME-03`: Homepage offers direct paths into products, solutions, and inquiry
- `ABOU-01`: Users can view a complete bilingual company profile
- `ABOU-02`: Users can find the Shenzhen office address and related contact facts
- `ABOU-03`: Users can view trust-building content, including company intro, partner brands, and reserved qualification areas

## Validation Approach

- Route-level rendering tests confirm the homepage and About-related content render in both locales
- Manual copy review confirms the first screen communicates company scope clearly
- Build verification ensures the logo asset, homepage layout, and new content model compile cleanly
- Later phase validation should include qualification placeholders and a fuller About page walkthrough
