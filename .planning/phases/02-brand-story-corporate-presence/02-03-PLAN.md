# Plan 02-03

## Objective

Strengthen the Phase 2 trust layer by turning placeholder-only trust signals into structured corporate modules across the homepage and About page, covering qualification readiness, authorization framing, and a readable delivery coordination path.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The homepage presents a visible trust section for qualifications, authorization readiness, and delivery proof
- The homepage explains a short coordination rhythm from scenario discussion into inquiry follow-up

- The new sections keep the same cardless Precision Industrial visual language established in Phase 1 and early Phase 2
