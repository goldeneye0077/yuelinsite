# Phase 2 UI Spec

## Visual Thesis

An engineering catalog cover translated into a modern corporate homepage: deep marine blue, cold steel neutrals, and a restrained amber accent used only where action or emphasis matters.

## Section Sequence

1. Hero: company name, business scope, CTA, real logo, one strong visual plane
2. Support: who the company serves
3. Detail: core strengths and business directions
4. Trust: cooperation brands and company profile
5. Final CTA: one consultation path with minimal friction

## Composition Rules

- The hero must remain edge-to-edge and visually dominant.
- Keep copy narrow and scannable.
- Use separators, lists, and editorial spacing instead of boxed card mosaics.
- Partner brands should read like trust references, not like decorative logos.
- Company profile content should feel grounded and factual.

## Motion Rules

- Hero elements may use one restrained entrance sequence.
- Logo or visual anchor may carry one slow atmospheric motion.
- Hover states should sharpen action, not become playful or loud.
